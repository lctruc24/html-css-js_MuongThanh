The story of
MUONG THANH

  • Muong Thanh is one of the largest and most reputable hotel chains in Vietnam. Starting with its first hotel in Dien Bien Phu, Muong Thanh has now expanded to nearly 63 hotels across Vietnam and parts of Indochina.
  • The hotel chain is divided into four segments: Muong Thanh Luxury, Muong Thanh Grand, Muong Thanh Holiday, Muong Than.
  • Each segment is designed to meet the needs of different customer groups, from premium to more affordable options. With locations in mountainous regions, coastal areas, and major cities, Muong Thanh offers modern, comfortable accommodations while embracing the unique spirit of Vietnamese culture.
  • Our goal is to deliver high-quality service, creating a warm and memorable experience for both domestic and international guests.
